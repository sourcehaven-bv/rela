title: Sync throw before togglingIndices.add bypasses dedup; double-click on malformed checkbox = two toasts
finding: toggleCheckboxInSource throws synchronously before togglingIndices.add. Rapid double-click on a `* [ ]` (which the toggler rejects) fires two `Failed to toggle checkbox` toasts. Either dedupe in the toast layer or move the .add before the try around toggleCheckboxInSource.
severity: nit
resolution: Moved `togglingIndices.add(index)` to BEFORE the try block that wraps `toggleCheckboxInSource`. Now the second click is suppressed by the in-flight guard before the synchronous toggler runs and throws. `togglingIndices.delete(index)` stays in the outer finally so the error path still clears the entry.
status: addressed
