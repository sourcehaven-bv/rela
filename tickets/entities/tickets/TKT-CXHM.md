## Problem\n\nOnce TKT-PNPI lands, the workspace has a `Tx` primitive and every writer goes through `ws.WithTx(...)`. But automation-triggered Lua scripts still re-enter the workspace via `scriptContextImpl` â†’ `workspace.CreateEntity` â†’ a **nested** `WithTx` that reuses the outer `writeMu` and the outer `repo.Transaction` via goroutine-local tx tracking. Nested-tx-reuse machinery disappears. The script context carries a `*Tx`, not a `*Workspace`.\n\n## Depends on\n\n- TKT-PNPI â€” provides the `Tx` type and the initial nested-tx-reuse implementation.\n\n## Scope\n\n**In scope:**\n\n- Extend `metamodel.ScriptContext` with a way to reach the current `*workspace.Tx`.
